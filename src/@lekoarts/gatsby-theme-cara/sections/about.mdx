## About DOMO

We are a international community of tech enthusiasts utilizing the most innovative tools to empower everyone. No roadmap, no bullshit, 100% community run.

## A decentralized Community

Instead of releasing a new social network that would most likely hardly be used, we focus on growing and educating a community across all existing social networks. We decide together and help each other to make use of the internet and not be consumed by it.
